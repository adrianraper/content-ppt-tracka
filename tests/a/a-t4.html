G’day and welcome to Perth!

Have you just moved here for your job? Perhaps you want to start a new life here with your family? Or maybe you are an international student at the university? We are here to help you feel at home in our city.

The Kookaburra Club is a social network of people who have come to live in Perth from other countries around the world or different cities in Australia.

We have a “Welcome to Perth” social event on the second Thursday of every month from 6:00pm - 7:30pm. All ages and backgrounds are welcome. It is a great way to meet people and make new friends and we always have a good time!

Come and join us for conversation, the chance to make new friends or find out more about everyday life in Perth. You can stay for five minutes or five hours!

See you soon!
