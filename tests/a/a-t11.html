Generate your own electricity

Wind energy is one of the most important new energy sources in the USA. Most of the energy comes from large commercial wind turbines, but more and more energy is being produced by small home wind turbines installed by individuals. Wind power is popular because it is clean and strong winds are not needed - even a little bit of wind will produce power.

Building your own home wind turbine is a fairly simple project which the average home owner can manage. In the long-term, wind power will save you money as well as reducing pollution.
