Introduction

The level test starts with 15 general questions.

Then you will do the main test (30-40 min). This has 3 sections:

  1. Grammar
  2. Vocabulary
  3. Reading

Please read the instructions for each screen carefully. The instructions tell you how many questions there are and how many screens there are. There is a timer on the right of the screen that tells you how many minutes you have left until the end of the test.

This level test is designed to give you a good indication of your English level. In order to assess your level accurately, it is important that you don't use the internet, dictionaries, reference books or ask anyone for help.

Your result will be displayed and can be printed when you have finished the test.

Now, click on 'Next' to start the test.
