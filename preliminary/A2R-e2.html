Reading Comprehension, Part 2
Read the following texts and then do items 35–38.

Text 1

You will receive the following email:

Dear friends
I am very sorry that I have to tell you that I will not be able to come to our monthly meetings anymore. It is really a pity that I won't see you anymore, but the reason is a happy one. I've got a new job! I'm going to work as a secretary for a big company. The company is in London, so I'm going to move there in two weeks. At first I am going to stay at a hotel until I have found a flat or a house to rent. I'm really looking forward to my new life in London, but of course I will miss you all very much. You must come and visit me in my new home!
With best wishes,
Laura

Read items 35–36. Decide if each of the statements in items 35–36 is true or not true.
  1. Laura is going to work in a hotel in London.

    1. true
    2. false


  2. She has already rented a flat in London.

    1. true
    2. false
