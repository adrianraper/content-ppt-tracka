Text 2

You are going to stay with the Whitby Walking Club and receive the following instructions:



Whitby Walking Club - Meals

You will get full details of mealtimes when you arrive. Up to six or nine people can sit at each table and you can sit anywhere you like. Bookings are not necessary. The waiters will ask you in the morning what you would like to eat from the dinner menu. For lunch on the walks you can choose from a lot of different foods after breakfast each morning. Whitby Walking Club has a bar which is open from before your evening meal until 11pm.

Read items 37-38. Decide if each of the statements in items 37-38 is true or not true.
  1. You must sit at a table that the waiter chooses for you.

    1. true
    2. false

  2. The bar will be open at 10 o’clock in the evening.

    1. true
    2. false
