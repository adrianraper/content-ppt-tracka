How to take this placement testers!

Time

This test lasts for 30 minutes. If you do not finish in that time, the test will automatically end.

Questions

There are sections on different English skills. Some questions will be easy and some difficult.

How to do it

Answer questions on the screen. When you answered as many as you can, use the submit button. Then you will go to the next section. You can't go back.

Result

At the end you will see your result. If possible you will get an email and you will be told what your school will do with the result.
